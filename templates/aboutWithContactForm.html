About

This project develops an intelligent configurator, including a BIM library offering collaborative BIM data accessibility, a similarity analysis between traditional projects and standardized products, and a generative design to optimize project configurations with modern methods of construction. In this repository we will store the sourcecode, test and efforts towards the development of the tool
